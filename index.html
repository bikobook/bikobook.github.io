Pet info

Justino

Justino is a very handsome male peacock, called Justin for short. His appearance is very striking; his plumage have many vibrant colors. He only eats the finest of foods, 'golden delight', 'silver goodness', etc. etc. (Those are the names of made up food.) He only allows the people of his refined taste, to pet him. His favorite hobby is strutting around, turning up his beak at anything not 'worthy' of his sight. He will learn to recognize your call but he is very absent minded (like his namesake), but will still be a perfect addition to your pets!

Giannabelle

Giannabelle is a white cat whose nickname is Gia. She is named after Gianna, my sister, (who needed a namesake. Her fur is very soft, warm, and beautiful, but one of her bad habits is she tends to shed fur all over the living room. She is a very quick runner, enabling her to catch even the nimblest of mice. She is always very sweet, and her purr is soft and pleasant to the ear. She has a condition called eczema, which I, (at the request of her namesake), won't tell you what it means. She is the loving mother of two kittens, Caterina and Vincento. You will hear about them later in this article. Her husband is Kolbe who I will also write about soon.

Kolbe

Kolbe is Giannabelle's loving husband and the father of two kittens, Caterina and Vincento. He is a brown persian cat, with soft, sleek, beautiful fur that he always cleans many times a day. He is very sneaky, always trying to surprise people, and his footsteps can be very light. Sometimes he can be quite stubborn, but is very affectionate.

Caterina

Caterina is a beautiful, gray, female kitten who's habitually called 'the darling'. She has very sharp instincts and can get surprised very easily. She really likes to eat food, (a little too much!) She is very vain, always wanting her fur brushed until it practically shone. She likes to play with her wind up mouse toy and chase it around. Once in a while she breaks it, treating it like a real mouse. She looks like a creature would look like if you cross a fairy with a kitten, with dainty white spots on her legs. She always loves it when she is carried around, (like a baby I know), and has the sweetest little purr.

Pio

Pio is Caterina's younger brother. He is very young, (and technically never ages.) He is always trying to hide in some out-of-the-way place and always stays in our backyard way longer than all the other cats. His eyes are very strange if you looked at her closely, one is blue and one is green. Despite that fact, he is still very handsome, his fur being a golden yellow. He is very picky, not eating everything on his plate, only very particular foods. He loves to nap, and play with a certain yo-yo and his nickname is Yo-Yo.

Christmatelle

Christmatelle is a dainty little hamster, colored ginger. Her favorite hobby is running around on her little wheel, and she is always escaping from his cage, and hiding beneath the living-room sofa! His fur is speckled with white, and it is very smooth and slick. Despite the fact she is a little too plump for a hamster, she will be the perfect hamster for you.

Vincento

Vincento is a magnificent rainbow colored parrot brought from the deepest jungles of South America. He always likes to copy people in the funniest way. However, whenever a stranger approaches and Vincento happens to be outside his cage, he is very defensive and pecks at the unfortunate passer-by. Despite that, he will still be a very good pet for you.
